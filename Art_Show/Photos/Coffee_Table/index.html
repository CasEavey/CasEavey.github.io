Coffee Table
Photo Collection

This is a coffee table that I built for my girlfriend, Hannah, as a Valentine's Day present in 2025. The tabletop is made from a wood slab, I don't remember what kind of wood it is, and the legs were purchased from Home Depot. This is the first bit of furniture that I have built and I'm very proud of it. We live together now and this is still the coffee table that you will find in our living room.
