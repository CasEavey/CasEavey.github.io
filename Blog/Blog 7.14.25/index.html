Why does this blog even exist?

July 14, 2025

This blog exists because I enjoy writing. This is not to say that I am a writer or that I am good at writing, all I mean by this is that writing is an enjoyable experience for me. At this point I suppose you could just stop reading but this is my first blog post so I want it to be good and I would appreciate it if you would keep reading at least a bit longer.

I've always thought that it would be interesting to have a blog, somewhere that I can post my thoughts and not expect anybody to read them outside of a few close family and friends, a public place that is all my own. Any time I found out that a friend of mine has a blog I've always been so impressed. Every month my girlfriend Hannah writes for The Post Calvin, a collection of daily essays written by graduates of Calvin University. It's absolutely amazing that she's able to write so much each month and all of her essays are phenomenal, I don't know if she would be okay with me linking to it but I'm going to and I'll take this part out if she objects. It's really inspiring to watch her do this each month and has been making me wish that I had an outlet for my thoughts too.

I keep putting paragraphs into this post, I think it makes me feel more professional. Another reason that I wanted to create this blog is because I just have so many things that I want to say. Every time that I go to a good restaurant, watch a good musical, or make something that I'm really proud of I want to share it with people, my only problem is that I don't have any social media. That isn't actually entirely true I have a Bluesky and an Instagram although I never use the Instagram and I don't actually know anybody else who really uses Bluesky. I have taken a lot of steps in the past 5 years to remove myself from social media algorithms as best I can and I'm not really interested in backpedaling on that just to be able to share my thoughts with strangers. The only thing I really miss is being able to look back on my memories.

I suppose the final reason that I wanted to start this blog is because I have an absolutely horrible memory. I literally struggle to remember what I was doing less than 24 hours ago. It's really sad to know that I'll never be the kind of person who can just remember what somebody was wearing when we first met. For these same reasons I feel like I could really enjoy getting into making photo albums. I just want something I can look back on and be able to relive the moments of my life that brought me joy, hopefully this blog can become that for me. Thank you for reading, hopefully you enjoy this blog as it continues to grow.
